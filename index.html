Brief introduction — TCOS 2021: Terms & Conditions of Supply (TCOS) 2021 set binding rules between distribution licensees and electricity consumers. They govern how service is sanctioned, connected and metered; categories and tariffs; contract demand and load changes; security deposits and power factor obligations; billing, payment, and disconnection/reconnection; meter testing, accuracy and replacement; supply quality, outages, and safety; theft/unauthorized use procedures; and grievance redressal. In Rajasthan DISCOMs, TCOS‑2021 superseded 2004 provisions and aligns with the RERC Electricity Supply Code, 2021 and the Electricity Act, 2003. Utilities must provide service standards; consumers must comply with technical, commercial and safety requirements for the life of the connection.
