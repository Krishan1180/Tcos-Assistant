About TCOS-2021 Document

Terms and Conditions for Supply of Electricity - 2021 (TCOS-2021) is a comprehensive regulatory document issued by Jaipur Vidyut Vitran Nigam Limited (JVVNL) that governs the supply of electricity to consumers in the JVVNL service area.

This document establishes the legal framework and operational guidelines for electricity distribution, covering all aspects of consumer-utility relationships including application procedures, connection policies, billing standards, service quality parameters, and dispute resolution mechanisms.

TCOS-2021 supersedes previous versions and aligns with the latest regulations from the Rajasthan Electricity Regulatory Commission (RERC), ensuring transparent, efficient, and consumer-friendly electricity services across Rajasthan's power distribution network.
