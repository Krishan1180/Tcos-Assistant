About TCOS-2021

Terms and Conditions of Supply of Electricity-2021 (TCOS-2021) is a comprehensive regulatory document issued by Jaipur Vidyut Vitran Nigam Limited (JVVNL) that governs electricity supply services in Rajasthan.

This document came into force on February 25, 2021 and covers:

  • New electricity connection procedures
  • Consumer rights and obligations
  • Billing and payment terms
  • Load extension and reduction processes
  • Connection transfer procedures
  • Safety standards and technical specifications
  • Dispute resolution mechanisms
  • Standards of Performance (SOP)

TCOS-2021 ensures transparent, efficient, and consumer-friendly electricity distribution services across JVVNL's operational areas including Jaipur, Kota, and Ajmer divisions.

About JVVNL

Jaipur Vidyut Vitran Nigam Limited (JVVNL) is a government-owned electricity distribution company serving over 32 lakh consumers in Rajasthan, India.

Key Facts:

  • Incorporated: July 1, 2000
  • Headquarters: Jaipur, Rajasthan
  • Service Areas: Jaipur, Kota, Ajmer, Alwar divisions
  • Parent: Rajasthan State Electricity Board (RSEB)
  • Services: Power distribution, billing, customer support

JVVNL is committed to providing reliable, quality power supply while promoting energy conservation and implementing government electricity schemes across its service territory.
