About TCOS 2021

The Terms & Conditions of Supply (TCOS) 2021 establish a binding framework between electricity distribution licensees (DISCOMs) and consumers in Rajasthan.

They oversee critical aspects of electricity service, including:

  • Service connection, sanctioning, and metering.
  • Tariff categories and contract demand.
  • Billing, payments, and disconnection procedures.
  • Meter accuracy, testing, and replacement.
  • Supply quality, safety standards, and outage management.
  • Procedures for theft or unauthorized use of electricity.
  • Consumer grievance redressal mechanisms.

Superseding the 2004 provisions, TCOS-2021 aligns with the RERC Electricity Supply Code, 2021, and the Electricity Act, 2003, ensuring standardized and fair practices.
